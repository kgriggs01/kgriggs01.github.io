Geyser & Fireworks Festival

by Keith Griggs

Come join us for the Soda Springs annual New Year's Eve Geyser & Fireworks festival.

The event kicks off at 11pm with free hot chocolate and cookies. A spectacular fireworks show will begin at 11:45pm with the finale happening at the same time the geyser erupts at the top of the hour – and ringing in the new year!

It’s a show you won’t want to miss!
