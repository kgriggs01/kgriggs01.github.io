Ocean beaches in the Rocky Mountains?

by Keith Griggs

Bear Lake is home to some of the best beaches the Rockies have to offer! Even toddlers can safely wade over a hundred yards out into warm chest-deep water, without the worry of an ocean’s riptide.

Bear lake offers numerous activities Summertime activities including boating, water skiing, wakeboarding, sailing, jet skis, paddle boarding, hydro flights, kayaking, watercraft rentals, ATV rides, camping, fishing and more!
