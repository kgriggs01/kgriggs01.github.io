Winter is finally here...

by Keith Griggs

So, that means it's time for exciting outdoor winter recreation. There are numerous winter activities to do near Preston Idaho including snow machining, down hill and cross-country skiing, snowboarding, ice skating, and ice fishing.

Preston isn't just fun in the winter becasue there is so much to do here year-round. When snow melts and the ground thaws, there are many ATV & mountain bike trails, camp & RV sites, hot springs, rodeos, mountains to climb, and lakes for swimming and fishing.

Preston is home to year-round outdoor adventure. If you are an outdoor enthusiest, then no matter the time of year, there is activity for you to enjoy here with us in Preston.
