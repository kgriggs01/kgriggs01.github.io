The main purpose of my site is to give users information about a few towns located in Southeastern Idaho. Towns include Preston, Idaho, Soda Springs, Idaho, and Fish Haven, Idaho.

The information included for each town will include the current weather, a weather outlook, and a town page for each location. The home page will have a current weather story and links to each town. Businesses and recreational option will be provided to try and draw tourists to the area.

There are many outdoor activities to enjoy in Southeast Idaho, including Bear Lake, Lava Hot Springs, Minnetonka Caves, and Pebble Creek Ski Area. The towns are also close to Utah State University, the home of the Aggies.
