Target Audience

The primary target audience for my site will be adults, of all ages, that are looking to spend time outdoors. I want them to visit my site when they are planning their next outdoor adventure.


Persona 1





Fictional Name: Jared Howard

Demographics: 19 years old. Single, but dates frequently. Lives with three roommates.

Occupation: Full-time student. Physical and Sport Education (Medicine) major.

Goals and Tasks: Jared is working toward his undergraduate degree and is currently a sophomore. Jared spends much of his time studying, but he also takes time to play as well. Jared loves the outdoors and enjoys going out on the lake to wakeboard and waterski. In the winter time Jared likes to snowmobile with his friends.

Quote: “Let’s hit the lake this Saturday.”


Persona 2





Fictional Name: Walter Lewis

Demographics: 67 years old. Married for 43 years. 4 children and 9 grandchildren.

Occupation: Retired Army Veteran

Goals and Tasks: Walter’s main concern is making sure his wife is happy. As a couple, they enjoy scenic country rides on his Indian motorcycle. Walter is an avid outdoors man that loves to hunt and fish. Walter looks forward to the weekend fishing trips with his grandchildren at nearby waters he frequents. Every year Walter hopes he will get that elusive bighorn sheep tag.

Quote: “This one’s a fighter!”


Scenerios


  • Scenario 1: Where can I get a new fishing lure? I need that new Rapala!
  • Scenario 2: Where is a place I can go to eat after a long day at the lake?
  • Scenario 3: What time is the festival next weekend?
  • Scenario 4: How far of a drive is it to Wyoming?
  • Scenario 5: How much snow are we expecting tomorrow?
